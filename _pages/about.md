About Me
======
I am climate scientist interested in understanding our past and our future. My educational background is in physics but have a strong interest in computational science.

Research Interests
======
1. Ice Age Climate Dynamics - Millennial Scale Climate Variability (MCV).
2. Anthropogenic Climate Change - Future tipping points.
3. Physical Oceanography and Global Biogeochemistry - The ocean as a mediator for long term climate change.
4. ML in Climate Science - New ways of emulating climate models and improving parameterizations.
5. CDR and CCS.
